Ground Floor Plan — Proposed Layout
Drawing Number: 2024-OL-03 Rev A
Scale: 1:50
Date: December 2024

This drawing shows the proposed ground floor layout of 12 Oak Lane following the construction of the single-storey rear extension.

The entrance hallway opens from the front door on the north elevation into a corridor running south through the centre of the dwelling. To the left of the hallway is the lounge, measuring 5.0 metres by 4.5 metres, giving a floor area of 22.5 square metres. The lounge has a bay window on the front elevation and a standard casement window on the side elevation.

To the right of the hallway is the dining room, measuring 3.8 metres by 3.2 metres. The dining room connects to the kitchen through an open archway.

The kitchen occupies the rear portion of the ground floor and extends into the new single-storey extension. The kitchen measures 5.0 metres by 3.7 metres, providing a floor area of 18.5 square metres. The kitchen features bi-fold doors on the rear elevation opening onto the rear garden, and a high-level window on the northern side elevation. Kitchen worktops run along the northern and eastern walls, with an island unit in the centre.

Adjacent to the kitchen is the new utility room, measuring 3.0 metres by 2.0 metres, giving a floor area of 6.0 square metres. The utility room is accessed from the kitchen through an internal door and has an external door on the western elevation leading to the side passage.

The rear elevation of the extension is set back 1.5 metres from the northern boundary fence. The western elevation of the utility room is positioned 3.0 metres from the western boundary wall.

A downstairs WC is located beneath the staircase, measuring 1.5 metres by 0.9 metres.

First Floor Plan — Existing Layout (No Changes)

The first floor comprises three bedrooms and one bathroom. Bedroom 1 is the master bedroom at the rear, measuring 4.5 metres by 4.0 metres with a floor area of 18.0 square metres. Bedroom 2 is at the front, measuring 4.0 metres by 3.0 metres with a floor area of 12.0 square metres. Bedroom 3 is a single room measuring 3.0 metres by 2.5 metres. The bathroom measures 2.8 metres by 2.5 metres with a floor area of 7.0 square metres.

Site Plan Notes:
The existing driveway provides two off-street parking spaces. The rear garden retains a usable depth of approximately 8 metres. The mature oak tree in the south-west corner of the garden is to be retained with a root protection area of 6 metres radius. A new soakaway drainage system is proposed in the rear garden to manage surface water from the extension roof.
